Telelectrix indulge in prismatic tones of electro-pop for their video ‘Strobe’

Before the world closed in mid-March, Telelectrix made a music video that was a pure feat of firsts...

Strobe Video Out Now!

The Massachusetts electronic-pop duo’s Slate Roof Films-produced music video is out today (September 18), two weeks before the track brings the dance party to streaming platforms October 2. With a colorful and vibrant music video and a sound to match, “Strobe” is an ode to nightlife in a year that has reduced it to intimate parties and digital hangouts. The anthemic track not only takes Telelectrix -- composed of the synth, songwriting, and production pair of Kate Lindsay and Steven Borek -- to new sonic heights, but also delivers a virtual dance party at a time when the world needs it most.
